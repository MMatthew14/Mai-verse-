About Bunny Board

Welcome to Bunny Board, a dedicated imageboard for fans of Seishun Buta Yarou wa Bunny Girl Senpai no Yume wo Minai (Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai).

This platform was created to celebrate and discuss the wonderful characters from this series, including:

  • Mai Sakurajima - The bunny girl senpai herself
  • Sakuta Azusagawa - Our protagonist with a sharp tongue
  • Kaede Azusagawa - Sakuta's adorable little sister
  • Shoko Makinohara - The mysterious girl from Sakuta's past
  • Rio Futaba - The genius scientist friend
  • Tomoe Koga - The tsundere underclassman

Feel free to share your thoughts, fan art, theories, and appreciation for these characters and their stories.

This is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with the official creators of Seishun Buta Yarou.
